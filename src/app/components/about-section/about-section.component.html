About me

Hi

I am a Fashion Designer and specialist in helping women connect with their essence and translate it into an authentic style, communicating with the world through their image. Through Image and Style Consulting and Personal Color Analysis, I help people express themselves uniquely, blending style, essence, and harmony!

My mission

My mission is to promote your self-esteem and well-being, highlighting what you can communicate through your image.

My goal is that, when you look in the mirror, you feel confident to face the world and express your personality. Together, we will build an image that tells your story and makes you feel ready for any moment in your life.

Values

Ethics, respect, authenticity, trust, empathy, valuing individuality, and commitment to transformation.
